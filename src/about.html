Literally Me

If I drive for you, you give me a time and a place. I give you a five-minute window, anything happens in that five minutes and I’m yours no matter what. I don’t sit in while you’re running it down. I don’t carry a gun. I drive.

I am a huge fan of Ryan Gosling and specially the movie drive and Bladerunner 2049. In fact he is Literally me (or I should say I am literally him), just kidding. Photography has always been fascinating to me and I firmly believe that Photography is all about capturing the moment the way it is, thus no need to have any large and shiny (expensive too) equipments. I created this website to curate some of my most favourite images across the internet (most of the images here are from Unsplash) and upload some of my captures as well. Explore the website and have fun!
